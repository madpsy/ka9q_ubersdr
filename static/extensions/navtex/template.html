NAVTEX Decoder - Decodes maritime safety information broadcasts

        
NAVTEX: Tune to 518 kHz (international) or 490 kHz (national) in USB mode. Use 500 Hz center, 170 Hz shift, 100 baud.

        
DSC: Tune to 2.188 MHz or other DSC frequencies in USB mode. Same settings as NAVTEX.

        
The decoder uses CCIR476 forward error correction to recover text even with weak signals.

        
Baud Error: Green bar shows positive error, red shows negative. Decoder automatically tracks and corrects timing.

QRSS is extremely slow CW read by eye off the waterfall. Set USB and pick a band above — signals appear as faint horizontal streaks (dots short, dashes long). The most activity is on 30 m within 100 Hz of 10.140 MHz. To see a signal more clearly: scroll to zoom the waterfall in/out (on both axes) around the cursor, drag to pan around, and double-click to reset. For finer real detail use Resolution (FFT size) and Span. The frequency axis shows real RF (dial + audio). Use Freeze then Save PNG to keep a copy.

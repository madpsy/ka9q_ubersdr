ℹ️ How to read this spectrogram: Each horizontal row = 1 minute of RF spectrum data. The image builds top-to-bottom throughout the day and will be fully populated after 24 hours (1 440 rows). The X-axis shows frequency (0–30 MHz for wideband); the Y-axis shows UTC time, with the most recent data at the bottom. Colour indicates signal strength — from noise floor (cool/dark) to signal peak (warm/bright) — as shown in the legend below. Hover over the image for exact frequency, time, and signal level.
